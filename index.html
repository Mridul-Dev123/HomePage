About me

Beneath a sky smeared with electric lavender clouds, the old carousel spun quietly in the middle of an abandoned fairground. Its chipped horses—paint faded by sun and time—still bore expressions of noble defiance as if ready to gallop into dreamscapes. A crow perched on the rusted ticket booth whistled a melody that sounded oddly like a forgotten lullaby. The air buzzed faintly, hinting at memories suspended in the twilight—half magic, half melancholy.
